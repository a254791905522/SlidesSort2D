Slide Sort 2D

Slide · shift · sort

Free iOS 15+ Puzzle Ages 4+

Slide, shift, and sort your way through fast-paced puzzle levels. Slide Sort 2D blends classic column-matching logic with a unique Middle Track: scroll infinite side columns, stash items in the center lane, and line up identical sets to clear the board before you run out of moves. Mine gems underground or serve dishes to workers—every stage has its own collection goal.

How to play

  1. Slide to align — Scroll the left and right columns up and down to arrange items.
  2. Shift & store — Use the Middle Track to move pieces between columns or hold them for later.
  3. Align sets — Stack matching items vertically to collect them.
  4. Reach the goal — Hit each level’s target to unlock the next challenge.

Middle Track mechanic

Strategy goes beyond classic sorting—the center lane is your bridge to big cascades and clutch saves.

Underground themes

Gem-mining adventures and underground food-service stages keep visuals and goals fresh.

Three challenge modes

Moves Mode, Time Mode, and Hybrid Mode tailor pressure to how you like to play.

Cascading combos

Chain reactions clear the board with satisfying particles and golden coin celebrations.

Boosters & hints

Undo, hints, and skip help you through tough levels when you need a nudge.

Daily rewards

Earn coins from levels and daily challenges to spend on boosters and extra moves.
